# Diwali Sales Analysis
 Diwali Sales Analysis
A Python-based Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project that uncovers customer buying patterns during the Diwali festival season using real sales data.

## Project Overview
This project analyzes Diwali sales data to identify key trends in customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and product preferences. The goal is to help businesses better understand their target audience and optimize their marketing strategies during the festive season.

## Project Structure
Python_Diwali_Sales_Analysis/
│
├── Diwali Sales Data.csv          # Raw sales dataset
└── Diwali_Sales_Analysis.ipynb    # Jupyter Notebook with full EDA

## Technologies Used

Python 3
Pandas – Data manipulation and analysis
NumPy – Numerical computations
Matplotlib – Data visualization
Seaborn – Statistical data visualization

## Analysis Performed
1. 👫 Gender Analysis

Most buyers are female
Females have higher purchasing power than males

2. 🎂 Age Group Analysis

Highest buyers are in the 26–35 years age group
Female buyers dominate this segment

3. 🗺️ State-wise Analysis

Top states by orders & sales:

Uttar Pradesh
Maharashtra
Karnataka



4. 💍 Marital Status Analysis

Married women are the most active buyers
They exhibit significantly higher purchasing power

5. 💼 Occupation Analysis

Most buyers work in:

IT Sector
Healthcare
Aviation



6. 🛒 Product Category Analysis

Most popular categories:

Food
Clothing
Electronics




✅ Conclusion

Married women aged 26–35 years from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors, are most likely to purchase products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories during Diwali.
